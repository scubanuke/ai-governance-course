# Module 0 — Orientation and the Founding Gap

## Learning Objectives

After this module, a student will be able to:

- Explain the "founding gap": why no critical-infrastructure sector but nuclear was ever required to define a formal design basis.
- Select the sector they will carry through the course and articulate whether a design-basis discipline is present in it today.
- State what the course will ask them to produce by the end (both design bases, applied to a real facility in their sector).


The hook and the setup. Why this course exists, and the single decision that shapes everything after it: **pick your sector.**

## Units, in order

1. `00-founding-gap.md` — the NIPP design-basis gap
2. `01-pick-your-sector.md` — choose the sector you'll carry through the course
3. `in-your-sector.md` — closing reflection
