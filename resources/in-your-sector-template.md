# In Your Sector — template

> Copy this block to the end of each core module (0–4). Keep it short. Its job is to make the learner apply the module's idea to the sector they picked at Module 0, before moving on.

## In Your Sector

You picked your sector back at Module 0. Take one idea from this module and make it concrete for that sector.

- **The idea:** *(one line — the module's central move)*
- **In your sector, that looks like:** *(prompt the learner to write two or three sentences)*
- **A question to hold:** *(a question this module's idea raises for their sector, to carry forward)*

*Write your answer somewhere you'll keep it. You'll draw on these reflections when you reach the capstone.*
